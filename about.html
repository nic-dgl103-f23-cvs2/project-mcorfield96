About Our Studio

Our Studio

Ascent Physiotherapy is a full service physiotherapy clinic located in Comox, BC. Our fully equipped studio is located just off Comox Avenue at 141 Nordin Street and features all the best amenities.

  • 12 treatment cubicles
  • Gym area with treadmill, stationary bike, and weights
  • Aerobics area with room for balance analysis and exercises.

Meet the Team

Therapists and Trainers

Kendra Mulligan

Kendra has co-owned Ascent Physiotherapy since 2010 with Ron Mulligan and is proud of the contributions that the clinic and staff have made to the valley by offering best practice, evidence based manual therapy and community involvement.

Kendra graduated from the University of Alberta in 1993 and started her career in a progressive private practice manual therapy clinic offering excellent mentorship in Grande Prairie, AB. She worked in Alberta for 18 years with 5 of those years owning a physical therapy practice. Kendra is active in the local mountain biking community and enjoys nature and exploring local, regional and North American mountain biking destinations.
