About Our Studio

Our Studio

Ascent Physiotherapy is a full service physiotherapy clinic located in Comox, BC. Our fully equipped studio is located just off Comox Avenue at 141 Nordin Street and features all the best amenities.

Our Facility

  • 12 treatment cubicles
  • Gym area with treadmill, stationary bike, and weights
  • Aerobics area with room for balance analysis and exercises.

Kendra Mulligan

Kendra has co-owned Ascent Physiotherapy since 2010 with Ron Mulligan and is proud of the contributions that the clinic and staff have made to the valley by offering best practice, evidence based manual therapy and community involvement. Kendra graduated from the University of Alberta in 1993 and started her career in a progressive private practice manual therapy clinic offering excellent mentorship in Grande Prairie, AB. She worked in Alberta for 18 years with 5 of those years owning a physical therapy practice. Kendra is active in the local mountain biking community and enjoys nature and exploring local, regional and North American mountain biking destinations.

Trish Leslie

Trish, a 2011 honors graduate from Queen Margaret University, Edinburgh, holds a BSc in Physical Therapy. Her diverse experience spans orthopaedics, women's health, geriatrics, neurology, and sports physiotherapy, including work with rugby players. With a focus on manual therapies, physiotherapy modalities, and exercise prescription, Trish has practiced in private and acute care since 2012. Certified in acupuncture, she continues professional development in orthopaedics and women's health, now enjoying life in the Comox Valley.

Jared Hromika

Jared, a 2014 UBC Master's of Physical Therapy graduate, brings diverse experience iin physiotherapy, while specializing in orthopaedics. His treatment approach combines movement assessment, manual therapy, and exercise prescription for enhanced mobility and reduced pain. Certified in Neurokinetic Therapy, he identifies compensation patterns, addressing the root cause of symptoms. Excited to offer bike fitting services, Jared, a triathlon enthusiast since 2006, completed additional training for rider assessment and bike fit during his UBC program. Beyond the clinic, he enjoys swimming, trail running, softball, hockey, and biking.

Shadi Fleifel

Shadi joined Ascent Physiotherapy in 2018. With a Kinesiology degree from Western University and a Physiotherapy Master's from Perth, Australia, Shadi brings over 10 years of physio experience. He worked with premier rugby clubs in Australia, James Bay Rugby Club, and the Canada Rugby 7's team in Victoria. Specializing in sports and general rehabilitation, Shadi continually enhances his skills through various courses. Outside the office, he enjoys family time, crossfit, swimming, biking, running, rugby, basketball, and surfing.
