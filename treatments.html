Our Services

Treatments

At Ascent Physiotherapy, we pride ourselves on offering the most effective, evidence based treatments available. All to ensure you are your healthiest self.

Manual Therapy

Manual therapy is a hands-on treatment where a therapist carefully moves the tissues and joints of the body to restore movement and relieve pain.

Intramuscular Stimulation

Intramuscular stimulation (IMS) is used to release or lengthen muscles by inserting very fine needle into the affected muscle to stimulate healing.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture involves inserting needles into the body to stimulate nerves in the skin and muscles to treat chronic pain and other conditions.

Radial Shockwave Therapy

Radial Shockwave Therapy is an FDA cleared technology proven to increase healing for soft tissue tendinopathy sending soundwaves into your soft tissue.

Athletic Taping

Athletic taping is the process of applying tape directly to the skin or over pre-wrap in order to maintain a stable position of bones and muscles.

Personal Training

Our trainers deliver safe and effective exercise programs for clients that increase strength, mobility and athletic performance.
